NAME: Kaja
ROLE: Consulting prep · degree decisions · startup

KPI: Cases practiced | 20
KPI: Decision deadline | May 2026
KPI: Business ideas evaluated | 10
KPI: First marketing posts | 5

PHASE: critical | This week — urgent | Start today

SECTION: Consulting case prep

ITEM: tag=product | effort=1 hr/day | Complete one full case per day
Use Case in Point or prepLounge. Time yourself strictly — 40 minutes per case. Write down what you got wrong after each one.

ITEM: tag=product | effort=2 hrs | Map your weak areas
After 5 cases, identify the frameworks you keep missing. Profitability? Market sizing? M&A? Focus the next week on those only.

ITEM: tag=content | effort=1 hr | Research your target firms' case styles
McKinsey, BCG, and Bain all have different formats. Know which you're targeting and find 3 example cases from each.

ITEM: tag=product | effort=30 min | Find a case partner
prepLounge or your university network. Aim for 3 sessions per week.

SECTION: Degree decision — GMAT vs CEMS vs double degree

ITEM: tag=product | effort=2 hrs | List your actual criteria
Write down what you want in 3 years. Score each option honestly against those criteria.

ITEM: tag=product | effort=1 hr | Research CEMS deadlines
Exact application deadline, required GPA, partner schools, language requirements.

ITEM: tag=product | effort=1 hr | Research double degree options
Which partner universities? Application windows? Overlap with consulting recruiting?

ITEM: tag=product | effort=1 hr | Speak to someone doing each path
One person per option. 20-minute calls. Ask what they wish they knew before deciding.

ITEM: tag=content | effort=30 min | Set a hard decision date
Pick a date. Put it in your calendar. Open-ended decisions never get made.

PHASE: soon | Business idea — this week | Evaluate and pick one

SECTION: Finding a winning idea

ITEM: tag=growth | effort=2 hrs | Generate 10 ideas using the right filter
Build with AI in days, solves a real pain, specific customer, can market next week. Write 10. Don't evaluate yet.

ITEM: tag=growth | effort=1 hr | Score each idea on 4 criteria
(1) MVP in under a week? (2) Know exactly who the customer is? (3) Can reach them on LinkedIn? (4) Would you pay for it? Score 1-3 each.

ITEM: tag=growth | effort=30 min | Pick the top 2 and stress-test
For each: who are the first 10 customers and how do you reach them specifically?

ITEM: tag=growth | effort=30 min | Make the final call
Pick one. You can pivot. You cannot build nothing.

SECTION: Build the MVP with AI

ITEM: tag=product | effort=3 hrs | Define what the MVP does in one sentence
Not a paragraph. One sentence. If you can't describe it in one sentence it is not specific enough.

ITEM: tag=product | effort=1 day | Build V1 using Claude or Cursor
Simplest possible version. Does not need to be polished. Needs to do the one thing.

ITEM: tag=product | effort=1 hr | Get 3 people to try it before marketing
One question only: did it solve the problem you promised?

PHASE: soon | Marketing — start next week | Before the product is perfect

SECTION: First marketing actions

ITEM: tag=growth | effort=1 hr | Write your first LinkedIn post
Not a product announcement. A story: the problem you noticed, why it matters, what you built.

ITEM: tag=growth | effort=30 min | DM 10 people who fit your target customer
Not a pitch. One sentence describing the problem, asking if they experience it.

ITEM: tag=content | effort=1 hr | Write 5 posts for next week — schedule them now
Problem post, solution post, behind-the-scenes, customer insight, personal story. Write all 5 now.

ITEM: tag=growth | effort=30 min | Set up a simple landing page
One headline, one email capture. Carrd.co is free and takes 20 minutes.

PHASE: later | Ongoing | Keep the momentum

SECTION: Weekly habits

ITEM: tag=product | effort=40 min/day | One case per day minimum
Non-negotiable during recruiting season.

ITEM: tag=growth | effort=1 hr/week | Review idea progress every Sunday
Is this still the right idea? What did you learn? What to do differently next week?

ITEM: tag=content | effort=2 hrs/week | Content and outreach block
Schedule it. If it is not in the calendar it will not happen.
